Responsible Disclosure

Seraphim Security welcomes responsible disclosure of security vulnerabilities. If you discover a vulnerability, we ask that you report it responsibly and give us time to investigate and remediate.


Reporting Guidelines

• Do not exploit vulnerabilities beyond what is necessary to demonstrate impact
• Do not access, modify, or delete data that does not belong to you
• Avoid actions that could disrupt services (DoS, brute force, etc.)
• Provide clear steps to reproduce the issue


Safe Harbor

We will not pursue legal action against researchers who act in good faith and comply with this policy.


Scope

This policy applies to assets owned and operated by Seraphim Security. Third-party systems are out of scope.


How to Report

Submit reports via encrypted email using our PGP key:

angelseraphim64 [at] gmail [dot] com

Include:
• Description of the vulnerability
• Steps to reproduce
• Proof of concept (if available)
• Impact assessment


Response Timeline

• Initial response: within 24 hours
• Triage: within 72 hours
• Resolution: depends on severity
